Who am I ?

My name is Rajat Shahrukh. I will show you how to survive in on of the most dangerous environment of our planet. I will be tested by many challenges in a place where mental sanity only last for a few hours without the good survival skills. I will visit one of the most dangerous places in the world: the Wild Code School. I will enter the Wild Code School for 5 months to see what it's like from the inside. I will try to learn HTML and CSS. I attend to classes about Git, GitHub and many more dangerous dark tools. I hope that the tales are true and I will have the honor to see the true nature of the Wilders on their field. I am a photograph and also an adventurer. I like to see complicated situations and find a way to overcome it. I like everything to be spicy, in the stove but most of all in the pictures I take. I am a man of action and I also a great adept of the spiritual world. Yes I meditate a lot! I chose the Wild Code School to show you an art that is hidden from the crowds, it is the Dark Wild Dev
