The Rooms

The brain room: Here is where the students come to connect their neurons if they are lucky to some. It is also a place where the most unexpected happens. Miracles sometimes and pure madness more often. Enter at your own risks.

The light room: Don't be fooled by the name of this room. Even if it is the most lit place in the school its occupant can't go very far on the dark side. Make a wrong step in this area and you won't come back the right path.

Here you can see the torture tools of the brain room. Of course we could not display some of those as they are bound to some secret techniques to storm the brains.

A wilder in action or who lost his mind in the light room. It's not always easy to establish. Let's say he found his way in the school ... maybe not the way out.
